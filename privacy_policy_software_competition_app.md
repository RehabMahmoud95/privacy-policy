# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 15/1/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **software competition** ("we", "our", or "us") operates with respect to user privacy. The App is designed for software competitions and **does not collect, store, or process personal information** from users.

By using the App, you acknowledge and agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. No Collection of Personal Information

We do **not** collect personal information of any kind. This includes, but is not limited to:
- Names
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Physical addresses
- User profiles or identifiers
- Payment or billing information

Users are not required to create personal accounts or provide identifying details to participate in competitions.

---

## 2. Competition Data Only

The App may process **non-personal, competition-related data** solely for functionality purposes, such as:
- Software submissions, code files, or project archives
- Automatically generated submission IDs
- Scores, rankings, or evaluation results

This data is **not linked to any identifiable individual** and is used only to run and manage competitions.

---

## 3. No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use:
- Cookies
- Tracking pixels
- Third-party analytics tools
- Advertising or behavioral tracking technologies

Any technical data required for basic operation is processed in an anonymous and transient manner.

---

## 4. Data Security

Although no personal data is collected, we implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect competition-related data against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.

However, no electronic system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

---

## 5. Data Retention

Competition-related data is retained only for as long as necessary to:
- Operate the competition
- Display results and rankings
- Ensure system integrity

After the competition ends, data may be deleted or archived in an anonymized form.
