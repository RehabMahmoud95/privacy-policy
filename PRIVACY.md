# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: January 2026_

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how the **Rosary App** ("we", "our", or "") handles user information.  
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

## Information We Collect
The Rosary App **does not collect, store, or share any personal data**.

Specifically:
- No account or login is required
- No personal information (name, email, phone number) is collected
- No location data is collected
- No contacts or files are accessed

## Offline Usage
The app works **completely offline**.  
All prayers, counters, and settings remain on your device.

## Third-Party Services
The Rosary App **does not use**:
- Analytics services
- Advertising services
- Cloud storage services
- Social media integrations

## Children’s Privacy
The app is safe for all ages.  
We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

## Data Security
Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is **no risk of data leakage or misuse**.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
Any changes will be reflected in this file with an updated date.

## Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the GitHub repository issues page.
